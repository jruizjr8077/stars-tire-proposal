Competitive Pricing Strategy

Unbundle the tire price from services. Win the comparison shop. Capture service revenue as visible, explicit upsells.

PRICING Unbundled Price Architecture Strategy

The Problem: Stars currently bundles mounting, balancing, and warranty into the tire price. This makes tires appear $60–80 more expensive per tire vs. TireRack, Discount Tire, and Amazon — even though out-the-door prices are comparable.

The Solution: Display tire-only price (lowest distributor cost + $20 markup) on product pages and Google Shopping. Services presented as transparent add-ons at checkout.

Three-Tier Product Architecture

Different product categories require different levels of automation. Retail runs fully automated. Commercial is semi-automated with paylinks. OTR and specialty stays quote-driven.

01 Retail / Light Truck Tier 1

Customer shops by size or year/make/model → adds to cart → unbundled services at checkout → pays via Clover or Acima lease-to-own → order routes automatically to lowest-cost distributor → same-day or next-day delivery to shop → customer picks up installed.

  • Year/make/model fitment guide
  • Real-time multi-distributor pricing
  • Google Shopping product feed
  • Unbundled service add-ons at checkout
  • Clover payment gateway
  • Acima lease-to-own financing
  • Abandoned cart recovery
02 Commercial TBR Tier 2

Customer requests quote via website form or phone → system checks Southern Tire Mart / ATD inventory via API → owner checks BZO manually for Landgolden/Atlas → builds quote in WP admin → sends paylink via SMS or email → customer pays through Clover hosted checkout → owner orders from distributor.

  • Commercial quote request form
  • Admin-side quote builder
  • Clover hosted checkout paylink
  • SMS/email delivery (Twilio)
  • Static TBR catalog (Landgolden + Atlas)
  • Fleet account invoicing option
03 OTR & Specialty Tier 3

Quote request form captures equipment type, tire size, quantity, fleet info → owner prices manually → paylink sent → customer pays → service scheduled.

  • OTR quote form with specialty size fields
  • Equipment type selectors
  • Manual pricing workflow
  • Paylink delivery via SMS/email
